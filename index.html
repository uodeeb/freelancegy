Introduction to the freelance world

Course outlines

1- session (A): Knowledge Base

A general terms and concepts needed by every freelancer to be aware of before getting his first step.

This includes:

  • Definition of main concepts.

  • A look at the complete process.

  • Freelance market worldwide.

  • Advantages and disadvantages of working on a freelance basis.

  • What you need to be a great freelancer.

  • Fields of freelance work.

2- session (B): Choose the field

A practical session helping learners to choose from different fields of work as freelancers.

This includes:

  • How to choose the field.

  • Project-based system, Service-based system, Contest based system.

  • Find your passion.

  • Brainstorming.

  • E-learning.

  • Where to find resources and classmates?

3- session (C): Work & Practice

We just determine our main field and this is the time for getting our hands dirty with some hard work and practice.

This includes:

  • Know better your field.

  • Use time management strategies.

  • Where to find specific field communities?

  • Tools and online support.

  • Work on your portfolio.

4- session (D): Test & Pivot

After a lot of work, it's time to see some results. Are you ready?

This includes:

  • Work volume vs time consumed.

  • Level of self-improvement.

  • Skills had been mastered.

  • How to analyze the progress?

  • Pivot or not?

About the course instructor



Mohammad Allam is a freelance online instructor front-end developer and UX designer. He started his freelance journey in 2015 learning different subjects from basic computer skills to advanced programming languages. He has been chosen lately to get the Google/ Udacity developer challenge scholar followed by a second Front-End Nanodegree scholarship & to be a trainer in One Million Arab Coders initiative. Mohammad is also a founder of Memix.online a website dedicated to help freelancers to learn for free & Egypt-now.tk a news website where you can read fresh news daily coming from the most reliable news providers like CNN, BBC, REUTERS, SKY NEWS ...etc.
